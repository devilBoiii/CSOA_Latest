Composition of Authority

The Authority shall, for the purpose of Section 9 of the CSO(Amendment) Act 2022, comprise:
  • A representative in executive positions from the;
    • Ministry of Foriegn Affairs
    • Gross National Happiness Commission
    • Department of Law and Order
  • Two representatives for the CSOs
  • The Director of the Authority, who shall be the member secretary.
